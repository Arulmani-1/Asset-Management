Assetra End-to-End Asset Optimization

Total Duration

Aproximately 16-24 months

Estimated Investment

Project budget $1.8M – $3.2M

Project Target

Reduce total cost of ownership by up to 35%
through lifecycle optimization

Overview

Assetra partnered with a large asset-intensive organization facing rising operational costs, unplanned downtime, and fragmented asset management practices. The client needed a structured, end-to-end approach to optimize asset performance while significantly reducing total cost of ownership.

Challenges

The organization managed a wide portfolio of critical assets with limited visibility across their lifecycle. Maintenance activities were largely reactive, asset data was scattered across systems, and long-term replacement planning was unclear. These issues led to higher maintenance costs, inconsistent performance, and increased operational risk.

Approach

Assetra implemented an end-to-end asset optimization strategy covering the full asset lifecycle. The engagement began with a comprehensive asset assessment to evaluate condition, criticality, and lifecycle cost. Based on these insights, a data-driven lifecycle strategy was developed, integrating predictive maintenance, performance monitoring, and long-term renewal planning.

Close collaboration with operational and maintenance teams ensured alignment between strategy and execution. Asset performance indicators were established to track improvements and guide continuous optimization.

Result

  • 40% reduction in Total Cost of Ownership
  • Significant decrease in unplanned downtime
  • Improved asset reliability and utilization
  • Clear lifecycle roadmap supporting future investment decisions
